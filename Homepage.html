OVERVIEW

Baderia Global Institute of Engineering & Management was established by Global Nature Care Sangathan (GNCS), Jabalpur to promote quality education in various Technical, Medical & Management fields. The trust was founded in 1999. The trust prides itself of establishing various educational Institutes since its inception.

Baderia Global Engineering & Management Alumni Association (BGEMAA) is an alumni association of former students (alumni) of Global Nature Care Sangathan’s Group of Institutions, Faculty of Engineering and Management, Jabalpur.
Under the Global alumni association (BGEMAA) every year alumni meet is organized in the month of December/ June. Every year we publish newsletters or magazines, and felicitate our bright alumnae. Through Newsletter alumni can publish their thoughts and view and can be associated with the organization. Also the information about latest development of their work is shared by them to know and inform about their extraordinary contribution in their area. Review meetings are organized time to time and to decide to whom to felicitate, and the accordingly decision is taken to award to be given in the name of Global Jewels. Fund Given by the student’s are utilized for the welfare of students for promoting them to take education those are needy and unable to complete because of family constraints.

AIM

Connect previous student and felicitate those who have done extraordinary contribution in their organization also utilization of funds raised for the social causes.

OBJECTIVE :

  • Gather all Globalians Under one umbrella.
  • Organize get together and cultural event.
  • Felicitation of the students for their extraordinary contribution in their field.
  • Deciding agenda about funds received from the students.
  • Utilization of funds for the poor and Needy student.
  • Association of student for the establishment of research activity if any.
  • Association of students for the welfare of society.
